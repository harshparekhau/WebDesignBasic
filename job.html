The main task is to sell the company make products and also generate leads for turnkey jobs. You will have to go to the sites and make study of the site and suggest them best solution. Job Description Key Skills

• Visit Site for Sales and Demonstration Purpose • Must have knowledge of KPI
• Regularly Visit Multiple Sites as per the requirement • Must have instrumentation industry sales experience
• Coordinate with the customer with site readyness and availibility to start work • OR must be able to demonstrate good skills
• Coordinate the dispatch and ensure that the material is delivered
• Follow ups with old clients and checking their product use after installation or use and also generate repeat orders
